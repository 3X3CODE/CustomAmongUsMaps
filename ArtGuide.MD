# Among Us Art Style
- this is probably some secret info that most AU artists wont reveal.
* Use thick outlines.
* Make sure your objects have a Main Color, a Shading Color (shadows) and an outline color.
* Make sure your colors match the purpose.
* When making darker colors, dont just lower the values on each RGB channel, you can shift them by a little to enhance the final outcome.
* This is based on preference, but my reccommendation is to not use a vector art program. You may instead use a pixel based one and use a larger canvas ( 3000px - 4000px, depends on room size ), draw one room at a time and put them together later.
# Map Background Characteristics
- To make sure your backgrounds and rooms look nice, ensure it has the floor and the wall.
- Make sure to have the horizontal wall height, horizontal door spacing, vertical door height and vertical door spacing the same throughout to you dont run into sizing issues when placing doors and other assets.
